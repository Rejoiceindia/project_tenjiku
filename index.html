Natural Food Forest Membership

Tenjiku Virtual Assets (TVAs) are your key to our membership privelages, aminities and Decentralised Farming Ecosystem.

What are TVA's?

Project Tenjiku will demonstrate first operation Decentralised Farming Ecosystem and TVA’s are essential components of the same.

TVA are easily tradable virtual assets also known as Tenjiku Virtual Assets (eg. NFT Contracts). They allow individuals to earn passive income creating an opportunity for inclusive growth via remotely maintaining rarewood tree plantation, livestock breeding (goats, poultry), beekeeping, mushroom cultivation, etc within our professionally managed farm infrastructure.

Along with the above TVA's grant exclusive acess to amenities, discounts, and other membership privelages which are further divided into 2 types.

Golden TVA - A Generational Asset

  • Type: Life Membership
  • Availability: Exclusively for Vacation Home Owners
  • Cost: NIL (with home purchase)
  • Expiry: Lifetime (until ownership changes)
  • Privileges: Voting rights, Access to amenities & discounts upto 50%, Ownership of Teak Tree, Opportunity for remote goat breeding, Opportunity to earn from professionally managed poultry farms.

Silver TVA - Tangible Passive Income

  • Type: Limited Period Membership
  • Availability: Anyone can invest.
  • Cost: ₹45,000 (one-time)
  • Expiry: On teak tree harvest or 12 years.
  • Privileages: Can resell membership/ TVA, Access to amenities & discounts upto 30%, Ownership of Teak Tree, Opportunity for remote goat breeding, Opportunity to earn from professionally managed poultry farms.

Membership Benefits & Perks

Exclusive Discounts

Gold Members, their family, and friends are eligible for discounts upto 50% on amenities and other community perks. While Silver Members are eligible for discounts upto 30%.

High-value Teak Plantation

Using a multi-layer 24x24 sustainable food forest model inspired by SPK & Miyawaki Food forest, teak wood trees are cultivated within our project. Each member gets to own and sell remotely, One high value Teak tree on its maturity (10-12 years). This provides members a long term opportunity to earn ₹1,00,000- ₹2,00,000 from mere one time investment of ₹45,000.

Annual Bonus Income Streams

Earn annually from remotely managed livestock. Breed up to 3 goats, 100 poultry birds (for a small monthly fee) and earn from their sale, creating consistent cash flow.

Resell & Liquidity

As a virtual asset (NFT), your TVA membership can be sold at a higher price in open marketplaces, making it a highly liquid investment.

Passive Income for Members

Long-Term (Teak): 100% to 200% return on original investment from sale of Teak wood on maturity.

Annual Bonus (Livestock): 8% to 24% return on original investment from add on like goat breeding, beekeeoing, poultry, mushroom cultivation.

Resale: Only Silver memberships can be resold for higher price in open marketplaces.
