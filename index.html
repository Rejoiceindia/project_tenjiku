About Tenjiku

Tenjiku, translated as “heavenly land”, is a first-of-its-kind project in India, offering a unique Indo-Japanese experience. We are creating an autonomous gated community of off-grid portable container homes with a natural food forest, and multiple inclusive investment opportunities to create passive income by investing in earth on our 50+ acre property.

Natural Food Forest Membership

Tenjiku Virtual Assets (TVAs) are your key to our Decentralised Farming Ecosystem and passive income opportunities.

What are TVAs?

TVAs are easily tradable virtual assets (e.g., NFT Contracts) that allow individuals to earn passive income. They create opportunities for inclusive growth via remotely maintaining rarewood tree plantations, livestock breeding, beekeeping, and more within our professionally managed farm infrastructure.

Golden TVA - A Generational Asset

  • Type: Life Membership
  • Availability: 100 (Given on Vacation Home purchase)
  • Cost: NIL (with home purchase)
  • Expiry: Lifetime (until ownership changes)
  • Privilege: Voting rights on what to grow and breed.

Silver TVA - Tangible Passive Income

  • Type: Limited Period Membership
  • Availability: 900 NFT Contracts
  • Cost: ₹45,000 (one-time)
  • Expiry: On teak tree harvest or 12 years.
  • Opportunity: Anyone can invest.

Membership Benefits & Perks

High-value Teak Plantation

Own one high-value Teak tree per membership, remotely. Potential return of ₹1,00,000 - ₹2,00,000 on a ₹45,000 investment after 10-12 years.

Passive Income Streams

Earn annually from remotely managed livestock. Breed up to 3 goats or 100 poultry birds (for a small monthly fee) and earn from their sale, creating consistent cash flow.

Exclusive Discounts

Members, their family, and friends receive up to 40% off on amenities and other community perks.

Resell & Liquidity

As a virtual asset (NFT), your TVA membership can be sold at a higher price in open marketplaces, making it a highly liquid investment.

Financial Estimates Overview

Long-Term (Teak): 100% to 200% return on original investment.

Annual Bonus (Livestock): 8% to 24% return on original investment.

Consistent monthly cash flows from maintenance fees benefit farmland owners and developers.
